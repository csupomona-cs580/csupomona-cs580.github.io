Player-Connect

Player-Connect is a place for gamers to meet up and find people to play games with.

We are solving below problems:

  • Unclear when friends are available to play online
  • Not all PC games are on the same platform (Steam, Battle.net, etc.)
  • Strangers are sometimes bad teammates
  • Strangers are sometimes loud and obnoxious
