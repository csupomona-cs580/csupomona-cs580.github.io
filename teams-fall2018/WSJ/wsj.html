PLAN SMART, SAVE GAS COST.

Wonder all the time where and when to fuel your car during daily commute or long trips? Our trip planner can help you find the cheapest route with over 150,000 gas stations in the U.S.

Plan your trip now!

GASME is an application which calculates the cheapest gas cost trip of the user's desired origin and stops considering the vehicle's gas comsumption and initial gas level and user's gas brand preference. The calculation uses A* with a computational optimization. The front end is a simple implementation with React. We are a group of developers who are inspired to solve the problems many commuters face. Read more about us below.
