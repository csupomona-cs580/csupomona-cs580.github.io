Our Project

  • This Intelligent Helpdesk will configure and add various types of additional fields such as single and multi line text fields, pick lists , numeric, date, and time to your custom forms.

  • This help desk will create custom Forms or Templates to handle incidents and service request.

  • It provides technicians and end users, role based access to specific custom forms.

  • It applies Fuzzy solutions in order to check and compare the proposed technique and then add the best solution in the Knowledge Base or compare helpdesk technician and give them a rank.
